Real Savior

Step into the shoes of a true hero in Real Savior, where the city needs your unwavering courage and sharp shooting skills! As enemy paratroopers descend from above, chaos engulfs the streets. Luckily, you have the power to turn the tide of battle. Take command of an armored army tank, your formidable weapon against the aerial assault. With precision at your fingertips, it's time to defend your home!

Prepare for heart-pounding action as you hone your aiming skills. Get ready to line up your sights and unleash a devastating barrage that will send your foes plummeting from the sky. Every shot counts, as the fate of the city hangs in the balance. Remember, each enemy that lands weakens the city's defenses, so it’s crucial to eliminate threats before they hit the ground.

In this explosive challenge, waves of escalating enemies will push your limits. Only the bravest and most strategic players will withstand the onslaught. Utilize your tank’s advanced firepower and quick reflexes to maintain the city’s integrity. Aim for high scores and victories, showcasing your prowess on the battlefield. Gather your courage and gear up—your city is counting on you!

How to play free Real Savior game online

To play Real Savior, simply use your mouse to control the tank's aiming and firing. Click to shoot at the descending paratroopers as they approach the ground. Be sure to keep an eye on your score and stay alert for tougher waves of enemies as you progress through the challenges!
